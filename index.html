✦ Our Journey

Where Every Chapter
Began

From a single glance to a thousand days of love — here's the story that became a book.

☕
Day 1 — March 14, 2022

The Accidental Meeting

We were strangers who ordered the same terrible coffee from the same tiny café. You said mine was better. I let you have a sip. We talked for four hours.

"I knew that day. I just didn't know what I knew yet." — her
🌙
Day 47 — April 30, 2022

The First "I Love You"

Under a power outage, surrounded by candles and half-played cards, he whispered it like it was the most natural thing he'd ever said. It was.

"She didn't say it back immediately. She just smiled. That was better." — him
🏠
Day 214 — October 14, 2022

Building a Home Together

We painted the walls three wrong colors before finding the right one. The imperfect white we settled on became our favorite thing about the apartment.

"Home isn't a place. It's whoever you're painting walls with." — both
🌸
Day 500 — July 27, 2023

Five Hundred Days of Us

We celebrated with a homemade dinner gone slightly wrong and dessert eaten straight from the pan. It was perfect in every way that mattered.

"We laughed more that night than some people do in a year." — her
📖
Day 847 — July 8, 2024

The Book Is Born

On a rainy Sunday, reading through old journals, we realized our story deserved to be held. To be dog-eared. To be read at 2am by someone who needed to know love like this exists.

"We wrote it for every couple who's ever felt unseen." — both
